60+ credible tools
7 layers mapped

The core finding: No single tool covers more than two adjacent layers. Layers 1 and 7 are oligopoly-mature; Layer 6 (voice AI) just crossed the usability threshold; Layers 2–5 are the valuable middle where $2–5B of category-defining value will be built in 24 months. GummySearch's shutdown (Nov 30, 2025) sent ~140K users fragmenting across 8+ successors with no clear winner. The Chinese market is deep on content signals, caught up on code agents, and essentially empty on Layers 2–5.

Layer 2 Summary — The Defining Opportunity

The pattern is telling: every Layer 2 tool is single-stream or shallow-multi-stream. PainHunt is the only one covering 3+ platforms with WTP-verb extraction.

The killer gap

No tool cross-references a Reddit complaint against a VC-funded startup working on the same problem, against a 1-star App Store review about an incumbent's failure, against a Google Trends spike. That synthesis is exactly what a founder needs and exactly what doesn't exist.

Layers 3–5 Summary — The Empty Middle
The single largest product gap

An indie-priced, solo-founder-facing tool that takes 100 raw signals from Layers 1–2 and outputs 5 ranked, hypothesis-formatted opportunity cards. This does not exist at any price below $500/month. Enterpret and Unwrap.ai both exceed $10M ARR proving enterprise demand, but the indie tier is completely empty.

Conclusion — The Stack Is Real but the Middle Is Empty

Layers 1 and 7 are oligopoly-mature. Layer 6 (voice AI) just crossed the usability threshold. Layers 2–5 are the valuable middle where $2–5B of category-defining enterprise value will be built over the next 24 months.


The Chinese market is deep on Layer 1 content signals, caught up on Layer 7 code agents, and essentially empty on Layers 2–5 — that is the arbitrage.


The single most defensible position: a Chinese-market Layer 1+2 tool that mines 小红书/知乎/豆瓣/B站 comments for founder opportunities, priced for solo developers, launched through 即刻 and 小红书. The second-most-defensible: the same tool in English targeting ex-GummySearch users. Everything else has a credible incumbent — but those two slots do not, and they probably won't stay empty much longer.
